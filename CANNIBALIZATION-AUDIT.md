## How to Avoid Future Cannibalization

- **Unique primary keyword per page** – Each page should target one distinct primary keyword.
- **Qualifiers in titles** – Use audience (brokers vs. borrowers), product (equipment vs. working capital), or role (CPA vs. consultant).
- **Vary meta descriptions** – Avoid identical or near-identical descriptions; include page-specific details.
- **Hub-and-spoke structure** – Use hub pages (declined-business-loans, equipment-financing) as primary targets; supporting pages should have narrower, long-tail focus.
