Who We Are

Commercial Finance Referrals is the referral program of Axiant Partners — a commercial finance firm working with business owners and the professionals who advise them across the United States.

Axiant Partners works in the part of commercial finance that traditional banks leave behind: businesses that are healthy enough to deserve capital but don't fit a narrow bank credit box, and the deals that get declined, stalled, or pushed aside because they're complicated. This site, commercialfinancereferrals.com, is how we open that work up to a wider network of referral partners.

The idea is simple. Thousands of professionals — loan brokers, ISOs, accountants, equipment vendors, consultants, and advisors — encounter business owners who need financing every week, often without a good place to send them. We give those professionals a structured, accountable channel: refer the opportunity, we evaluate it and work to match it to financing, and when a deal closes, you share in the revenue. The brand name says exactly what it is. The tagline, "powered by axiant," points to the firm behind it.

What We Do

Our focus is the financing that's harder to place. When a borrower has been declined by a bank, carries weaker credit, operates in a restricted industry, needs to move faster than a bank can, or simply falls outside a single lender's program, we work to find a path. That spans second-look placement for declined deals, equipment financing, working capital, accounts receivable financing, SBA and commercial real estate, and — for over-leveraged businesses — MCA debt relief and consolidation options.

For the referral partner, the role is deliberately light: you make the introduction, and we handle evaluation, lender matching, and the legwork of moving a deal forward. You are never asked to broker the loan, underwrite, or carry the process yourself. We keep you informed, and when a deal funds, you're compensated per your agreement. The full picture of how compensation works is covered on how referral fees work.

Who We Serve

If you work with business owners, you likely encounter financing needs you have no clean way to act on. The referral program is built for exactly those professionals.

  • Loan brokers & ISOs—place the deals that fall outside your usual lenders, including over-leveraged and stacked MCA merchants you can't fund.
  • CPAs & accountants—see clients' cash-flow needs first; the CPA referral program turns that visibility into value.
  • Equipment vendors & dealers—close more sales by offering financing through the vendor referral program.
  • Consultants & advisors—help clients solve funding and debt problems via the debt consolidation referral program.
  • Attorneys & turnaround professionals—offer distressed clients an alternative to evaluate before bankruptcy.

What We Are — and What We Are Not

Honesty about scope is part of how we operate. Here is the plain version, so you and your clients always know what to expect.

We are not a direct lender. We do not issue loans or make funding decisions. We connect a referral partner's opportunity with financing sources and work to identify a path. Lenders decide whether and how to fund, and no outcome is guaranteed. Every deal is evaluated on its own merits.

We are not a law firm or financial advisor. The guides on this site explain financing concepts and referral practices in general, educational terms. They are not financial, legal, tax, or bankruptcy advice. Business owners — especially those weighing debt relief or restructuring — should review their own situation and consult appropriate licensed professionals before deciding.

We do not promise specific rates, terms, or approvals. Where this site cites typical ranges, they are illustrative and depend on the lender, product, credit, and structure of each deal. Referral compensation follows funded deals under a signed referral agreement, not introductions alone. We think being clear about all of this is what earns a partner's trust the second time, not just the first.

FAQ

Questions about Axiant Partners

Who is behind Commercial Finance Referrals?

Commercial Finance Referrals is operated by Axiant Partners, a commercial finance firm based in the United States. The site is the referral arm of the business: it gives brokers, CPAs, vendors, consultants, and advisors a structured way to refer business financing opportunities and share in revenue when deals close.

Is Axiant Partners a lender?

No. Axiant Partners is not a direct lender. We facilitate introductions between referral partners' clients and financing sources, and we evaluate opportunities to identify possible funding paths. Funding decisions are made by lenders, and no outcome is guaranteed.

How do you make money?

When a referred deal closes, compensation is shared with the referral partner under a signed referral agreement—commonly around 35% revenue share. Our model is built around successful placements, which aligns our interests with both the referral partner and the business owner who needs funding.

Who can refer deals?

Commercial loan brokers, ISOs, CPAs and accountants, equipment vendors, business consultants, financial advisors, attorneys, and other professionals who work with business owners. You don't need a lending license to refer. A signed referral agreement is required before submitting deals.
